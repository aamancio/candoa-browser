# Candoa Trademark Policy

This policy explains how the Candoa name, icon, logo, app identity, and related
brand assets may be used.

The Candoa source code is licensed under the Mozilla Public License 2.0. That
source-code license does not grant permission to use the Candoa trademarks,
brand assets, app identity, signing identity, update feed, website identity, or
public distribution identity.

## Official Candoa Builds

Only builds published by the Candoa maintainers may be called "Candoa" or use
the Candoa icon, logo, bundle identity, update feed, website identity, or other
official brand assets.

An official Candoa build is a build that is signed, notarized, distributed, or
otherwise explicitly published by the Candoa maintainers.

## Forks and Modified Builds

You may fork, modify, build, and distribute the source code under the terms of
the Mozilla Public License 2.0, but modified or independently distributed builds
must use a different name, icon, bundle identifier, update feed, and public app
identity.

Forks and modified builds should not imply that they are official Candoa builds,
endorsed by Candoa, or maintained by the Candoa maintainers.

Acceptable descriptions include:

- "A fork of Candoa"
- "Based on Candoa"
- "Built from Candoa source code"

These descriptions should not be used more prominently than the fork's own name.

## Permitted Community Use

You may use the Candoa name in a factual, non-confusing way to discuss,
document, review, compare, or contribute to the project.

Examples:

- Referring to the upstream project as Candoa
- Linking to the official Candoa repository or website
- Saying that a project is compatible with, derived from, or inspired by Candoa

## Not Permitted Without Written Permission

Without written permission from the Candoa maintainers, you may not:

- Distribute a modified app using the Candoa name or icon
- Use Candoa branding in a way that suggests an unofficial build is official
- Use the Candoa name, icon, logo, or app identity for a competing app store
  listing, website, social account, package, installer, update feed, or release
  channel
- Register domains, accounts, package names, bundle identifiers, or marks that
  are confusingly similar to Candoa

## Reporting Confusing Use

If you see a build, website, package, or account that appears to impersonate
Candoa or confuse users about whether it is official, please report it through
the project's issue tracker or contact the maintainers.
